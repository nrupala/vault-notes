# 📔 Vault Notes: High-Security Knowledge Usage

Vault Notes is a fortress for your most sensitive thoughts and structured intelligence.

### 1. 🔑 Seed Phrase & Credential Archive
* **Scenario**: Storing 24-word recovery phrases for crypto-wallets and hardware security keys.
* **Why Vault Notes**: Safer than "Notes" apps that sync to Apple/Google. The local-only encryption ensures your secrets never touch the wire in plaintext.

### 2. 🗞️ Investigative Journalism & Whistleblowing
* **Scenario**: Archiving source contacts, timeline drafts, and sensitive document summaries.
* **Why Vault Notes**: Zero-trust architecture protects both the journalist and the source. Works offline in high-risk environments.

### 3. 🧠 Deep Mirroring (Private Journaling)
* **Scenario**: Daily reflection on mental health, trauma recovery, or unfiltered stream-of-consciousness.
* **Why Vault Notes**: Total psychological safety. Knowing your rawest thoughts are encrypted and stored solely on your device allows for true honesty.

### 4. 🏢 Corporate IP & Strategy Drafts
* **Scenario**: Drafting pre-patent technology specifications or M&A strategy.
* **Why Vault Notes**: Prevent "insider threat" leakage and corporate espionage from centralized cloud vulnerabilities.
